  \noindent\textbf{Intro to complex numbers}\\
  Basic review of complex numbers. Proving basic properties that you are
  familiar with from basic high school pre-calculus. Exponential form of complex
  \hspace*{\fill}

  \vspace{10pt}
  \noindent\textbf{Complex functions}\\
  Study the difference between real and complex functions. The difference
  between real and complex derivatives. The Cauchy-Riemann equations. Basic
  complex functions, such as sin, cos, exp, log, and their derivatives, along
  with different logarithmic branches.\hspace*{\fill}

  \vspace{10pt}
  \noindent\textbf{Complex integration}\\
  Study contour integration, Cauchy's integral theorem, and Cauchy's integral
  formula. The residue theorem and its applications. The argument principle and
  Rouche's theorem. The winding number and its applications. The maximum modulus
  principle. The open mapping theorem. The Riemann mapping
  theorem.\hspace*{\fill}

  \vspace{10pt}
  \noindent\textbf{Power series}\\
  Study Taylor series and Laurent series. The convergence of power series. The
  radius of convergence. The Weierstrass theorem. The Cauchy product of power
  series. The Hadamard product of power series. The Abel theorem. The Borel
  theorem. The Mittag-Leffler theorem. The Weierstrass factorization theorem.
  The Casorati-Weierstrass theorem. The Runge theorem.\hspace*{\fill}
